## üåü Overview

Zenith is a comprehensive digital planner designed for working professionals who want to achieve more while maintaining work-life balance. Inspired by premium tools like Sunsama, Motion, and Amazing Marvin, Zenith combines guided daily rituals, calming visual design, and intelligent tracking‚Äîall in a free, GitHub-hostable package.

### Philosophy

> "Stress reduction comes from intentional limitation, not feature abundance."

Zenith helps you do **less with more intention**. Instead of optimizing for maximum output, it focuses on sustainable productivity through realistic capacity planning, protected personal time, and mindful daily rituals.

## üìñ How to Use

### First-Time Setup

When you first open Zenith, you'll go through a quick onboarding:

1. **Enter your name** - Personalizes your experience
2. **Set work hours** - Defines your working window (default: 9 AM - 5 PM)
3. **Choose daily capacity** - How many hours of deep work you can sustain (default: 5 hours)

### Daily Workflow

#### Morning Ritual (5-10 minutes)

1. **Set your Daily Highlight** 
   - Ask yourself: "What would make today feel successful?"
   - Enter one main focus at the top of the daily view
   - This should be your MIT (Most Important Task)

2. **Add Today's Tasks**
   - Click "Add Task" or press `T`
   - Set priority (High/Medium/Low)
   - Choose category (Work/Personal/Health/Learning)
   - Estimate duration in minutes
   - Watch your capacity indicator‚Äîstay under 100%!

3. **Time Block Your Day**
   - Click "Add Block" to schedule focused work time
   - Protect time for deep work, meetings, and breaks
   - Visual blocks help prevent overscheduling

4. **Check In**
   - Rate your energy (1-5)
   - Log your mood (üò´ to üòä)
   - Note your sleep quality

#### During the Day

- **Complete tasks** by clicking the checkbox
- **Start Focus Mode** by clicking the play button on any task
  - 25-minute Pomodoro timer (customizable in Settings)
  - Minimalist interface to reduce distractions
  - Task auto-completes when timer ends

#### Evening Shutdown Ritual

1. Click **"Begin Shutdown Ritual"** at the bottom of the daily view
2. **Review accomplishments** - Celebrate what you completed
3. **Reflect on wins** - Write down what went well
4. **Handle incomplete tasks** - Move to tomorrow or delete
5. **Plan tomorrow** - Set tomorrow's highlight and top 3 priorities
6. **Close your day** - See your summary stats

### Weekly Planning

Navigate to the **Week** view to:

- Set **Weekly Objectives** (3-5 key outcomes)
- See tasks across all 7 days
- Review weekly stats
- Use arrow buttons to navigate between weeks

### Monthly Planning

The **Month** view provides:

- Calendar with task indicators
- Monthly goals tracking
- Quick navigation to any day
- Month-at-a-glance statistics

### Yearly Review

The **Year** view shows:

- **Year in Pixels** - Mood data visualized across 365 days
- **Annual Goals** - Track big-picture objectives
- Progress bars showing completion percentage

### Habit Tracking

Navigate to **Habits** to:

- Create new habits (daily, weekdays, or weekly)
- Toggle completion for the last 7 days
- Track current and longest streaks
- Build positive routines that stick

### Goals & Life Balance

The **Goals** view organizes your goals into six life areas:

- üíº **Career** - Professional growth and work achievements
- üí™ **Health** - Fitness, nutrition, and wellness
- ‚ù§Ô∏è **Relationships** - Family, friends, and social connections
- üí∞ **Finance** - Savings, investments, and financial goals
- üìö **Learning** - Education, skills, and personal development
- üé® **Creativity** - Hobbies, projects, and creative pursuits

This prevents the common trap of optimizing career while neglecting other areas.

### Analytics & Insights

The **Insights** view helps you understand your patterns:

- **Productivity Score** - Combined metric (task completion + habits)
- **Task Completion Chart** - Visual trend over time
- **Energy/Mood Patterns** - Identify your best times
- **Category Breakdown** - See time allocation
- **Current Streaks** - Top habit streaks

## ‚öôÔ∏è Settings

Access Settings via the gear icon in the sidebar:

- **Profile**: Update your name
- **Work Hours**: Adjust your working window
- **Daily Capacity**: Change focus hours limit
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Customize focus/break durations
- **Data**: Export, import, or reset your data

### Data Backup

Zenith stores all data in your browser's localStorage. To back up:

1. Go to Settings
2. Click **Export Data**
3. Save the JSON file

To restore:

1. Go to Settings
2. Click **Import Data**
3. Select your backup file

## üí° Tips for Success

### Start Small
- Begin with just the Daily view for the first week
- Add habits and goals gradually
- Don't try to use every feature at once

### Be Realistic
- Most people can do 4-5 hours of deep work per day
- The capacity warning is there to help you
- It's okay to move tasks to tomorrow

### Protect Personal Time
- Use time blocks for lunch, exercise, family
- Work-life balance isn't automatic‚Äîplan for it
- The shutdown ritual creates psychological closure

### Review Weekly
- Every Sunday or Friday, look at the Week view
- Celebrate wins, learn from challenges
- Set fresh objectives for the coming week

### Track Mood
- Daily check-ins reveal patterns over time
- Low energy? Maybe you need more sleep
- The Year in Pixels shows long-term trends
